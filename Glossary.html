Glossary


Ezekia: pronounced ee-zeek-ee-ah. the earth empire capital, and main city. The city of earth.

Ixoyonia: pronounced Icks-oh-yo-nee-a. The flower that is often rare to be seen, and often only grows in forests. Is extremely hard for an individual to grow this flower by magic and is seen as a great achievement when mastered. This flower may be used in a few very complex medicines that are almost never made due to rare sightings. Is often a symbol of hope.

Kecyl: pronounced Key-sill. One of the main characters and is chosen by the spirit of the element earth to be the true representative of the earth. Full name is Kecyl Jude Folook. Is 18 years of age in the timespan of the story and is 6 feet and 1 inch tall. Weight is 169.5 pounds, and is a wolf Kuhamut. Hisbirthday in the story universe is Anthovei 80, 805TCD; but when it comes to translation his birthday is May 1st. The link to his character page is here.

Kuhamut: pronounced koo-ha-mut. The technical term for the species that shifts into an animal form and is considered third class. A kuhamut can be any existing animal, as an example is that Kecyl is a wolf kuhamut.

Pestelgia: pronounced Pest-ee-el-gee-ah. The planet in which all habitants live and where the story takes place.

Ponunnia: pronounced pon-yune-nee-a. The village that is associated with plant magic. Also known as Kecylâ€™s home village.

Sennalhi: pronounced Sen-nall-hee. The little sister to Kecyl that was born when he was 10 years of age. Her full name is Sennalhi Cella Folook. A character who never met her mother since she died at childbirth. Is a wolf kuhamut.The link to her character page is here.

Torvohn: pronounced Tor-von. The currency used world wide for trade. There are three types; copper, silver, and gold. 35 copper= 1 silver, 27 silver= 1 gold.
