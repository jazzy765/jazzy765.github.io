Calendar


In one year, the world of Pestelgia has 4 months(1 for each season). Every month has 96 days except for the last one.


Eras

EC era: (Elemental Creation) after the elemental gems were created and population began to occur. 3,000 years long (1-3,000EC).

FCD era: (first chosens’ death)It occurs after 4 of the 5 first chosen ones passed away. 1,500 years long (1-1,500FCD).

SCD era:(Second chosens’ death) after 4 of the 5 chosen ones passed away. 1,000 years long (1-1,000 SCD).

TCD era:(third chosens’ death) it occurs after 4 of the 5 third chosen ones passed away. Current era at 823 TCD.
